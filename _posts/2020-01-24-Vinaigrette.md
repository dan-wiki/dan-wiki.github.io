## Ingredients



*(Dresses \~2 cups of salad)*



-   1/2 tsp Dry savory

-   1 tbps Extra virgin olive oil

-   1/2 tsp Dijon mustard

-   1/2 tsp Lemon juice

-   1/8 tsp Rice vinegar

-   1/8 tsp Worcestershire sauce

-   1/8 tsp Fish sauce

-   1/8 tsp Salt

-   1/8 tsp Pepper



## Instructions



-   Wisk everything together (a fork will usually suffice)
