## Ingredients

-   1 lb ground pork

-   2 tsp kosher salt

-   1 tsp ground pepper

-   1 tsp ground majoram

-   1 tsp sage

-   1 tsp fennel seed

-   1/8 tsp cayanne

-   1/4 tsp garlic powder

-   1/4 tsp crushed red pepper



## Directions

-   Combine in a bowl and mix in KictchenAid

-   form into patties

-   fry in cast iron pan using oil
