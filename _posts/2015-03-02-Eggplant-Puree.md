## Ingredients

-   2 lb eggplant

-   1/4 cup almond meal

-   1/4 cup butter

-   1/4 Parmesan cheese

-   1/4 Cheddar cheese

-   1/2 cup heavy cream

-   juice of 1 lemon

-   1/2 tsp salt

-   1/8 tsp pepper

-   1/2 tsp nutmeg to taste



## Directions

1.  Roast Eggplant whole on 450F for 15 minutes

2.  Cut off stem and bottom

3.  Scrape eggplant into food processor or use whole eggplant cut into

    chunks

4.  Add all other ingredients

5.  Puree
