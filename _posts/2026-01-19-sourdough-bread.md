## Starter

- 50g whole wheat flour  
- 50g water  

**Steps:**
1. Add to beaker and mix.
2. Remove half after 24 hours and add back in with equal parts flour and water.
3. When it rises by half consistently, it's ready to use.
4. Feed daily at room temp, or store in fridge and feed weekly.

---

## Bread Levain

- 45g starter  
- 45g stone-ground whole wheat flour  
- 45g unbleached all-purpose flour  
- 90g filtered water at room temperature (~73°F)

**Steps:**
1. Stir together ingredients in a jar. (You can use starter strait from the fridge.)
2. Place near a 78°F heat source.
3. Wait 6 hours.

---

## Bread Dough

- 500g unbleached all-purpose flour  
- 275g unbleached bread flour  
- 175g stone-ground whole wheat flour  
- 660g warm water (90–95°F)  
- All of the levain from above

**Steps:**
1. Hand mix all ingredients except starter until fully hydrated. Do not knead. Wait 1 hour.
2. Add levain and spread across dough with wet hands. Dimple in with fingers and pinch to incorporate.
3. Scoop and slap for 2 minutes using Rubaud method.
4. Cover bowl with towel and wait 20 minutes
5. Add a 1/4 90-95F cup water and 18g sea salt. (you can microwave water for 15 second bursts and use thermometer)
6. Dimple ans incorporate like before then slap, turn and fold over until it becomes smooth in 3-5 minutes.
7. Bulk ferment in bowl at 78°F for 3–4 hours until doubled.

---

## After Bulk Ferment

5. **Fold Set**:
   - Fold and rotate dough in bowl 4 times
   - Wait 15 min → fold again  
   - Wait 15 min → fold again  
   - Wait 30 min → fold again  
   - Then let rest for 3–4 hours

6. Carefully transfer dough onto flourless surface.  
7. Divide in half and pre-shape into round balls.  
8. Cover with overturned glass bowl for 15 minutes.  
9. Let sit uncovered for 10 minutes.  
10. Flour bannetons with rice flour.  
11. Dust dough balls with regular flour.  
12. Flip dough, roll like a burrito: stretch sides, coil and tighten by pulling inward.  
13. Place in bannetons seam side up.  
14. Cover with plastic or zip bag secured with rubber band.  
15. Refrigerate for 14–15 hours.

---

## Baking

16. Place Dutch oven in cold oven and preheat to 500°F for 1 hour.  
17. Dust bottom of loaf with cornmeal.  
18. Flip loaf onto parchment paper
18. Score top in a cross-hatch pattern.  
19. Place loaf on parchment paper into Dutch oven.  
20. Bake 20 minutes with lid on.  
21. Reduce temp to 475°F and remove lid. Bake another 25–30 minutes.  
22. Let rest 1 hour before slicing.
23. Store outside the fridge in s bread bag.
