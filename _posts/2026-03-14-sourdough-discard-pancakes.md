### Ingredients

- 1 cup sourdough discard (straight from the fridge)
- 1 large egg
- ¼ cup milk (or water if needed)
- 1 tablespoon melted butter or oil (optional)
- Pinch of salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda

**Optional**

- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Cinnamon
- Vanilla
- Bacon bits

---

### Method

1. **Mix the batter**

   Add the sourdough discard, egg, milk, melted butter (if using), salt, and baking soda to a bowl.  
   Whisk until combined. Lumps are fine.

2. **Rest**

   Let the batter sit for about 5 minutes. Small bubbles may form.

3. **Heat the pan**

   Heat a nonstick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly butter or oil if desired.

4. **Cook the pancakes**

   Spoon about ¼ cup batter per pancake onto the pan.  
   Cook until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges look set.

5. **Flip**

   Flip and cook for about 1 more minute, until golden.

6. **Serve**

   Stack and serve with butter, maple syrup, or your favorite toppings.
