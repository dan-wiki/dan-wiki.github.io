## Ingredients

-   6oz gin
-   .5oz olive juice
-   2 green olives
-   1oz ice cubes
-   Shaker
-   Strainer
-   Stirring spoon
-   Martini glass

## Instructions

1.  Optionally add ice and water to the Martini glass and pour out
    before pouring in drink
2.  Put everything except olives into the shaker
3.  Stir until cold
4.  Pour using strainer into Martini glass
5.  Add in olives
